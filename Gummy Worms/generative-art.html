Gummy Worms

Gummy Worms is a contemporary generative art project inspired by the playful, colorful world of gummy worm candies and the groundbreaking Chromie Squiggles art series. The collection bridges the gap between the nostalgia of childhood in the 90s and the digital realm of millenials in adulthood. It is a celebration of the transition from real-life fun to the boundless possibilities of the onchain era.

This collection is a debut generative project by LGHT.
